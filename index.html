Climate Change

SOS! Save Our Planet

save our planet global summit 2022

Global temperatures and sea levels are rising, and possibly contributing to larger more devastating storms. This can all be contributed to climate change. Climate change is defined as gradual changes in all the interconnected weather elements on our planet over approximately 30 years. The data shows the Earth is warming and it's up to us to make the changes necessary for a healthier planet.

A joyful celebration believing in the value of openness and sharing, creating a positive change with people from over 80 countries is taking place in August, in Paris.

2022.08.15(MON) ~ 16(TUE)

@ Conference Hall, Paris

Main Program

  • Lecture

    Listen to popular speakers from various countries about the next steps and necessary actions the world has to take to fight climate change.

  • Exhibition

    Appreciate various efforts made so far towards the fight against climate change. Showcase marvelous works in that light.

  • Forum

    Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with experts for each topic. Ask and answer popular questions.

  • Workshop

    Examine new ways of conquering climate change by checking for safer or more efficient energy production techniques.

  • Networking

    Get amazing opportunities to network with SOP affiliates around the world, also after the summit. Work better together.
